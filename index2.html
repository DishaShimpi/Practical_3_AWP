Some Essentials to Help You Speak With More Confidence:

  • Begin by addressing the audience.
  • Pause, Smile and count to three before saying anything.
  • Transform nervous energy into enthusiasm.
  • Find something that calms you down when you're nervous.

10 Commandments of Confident Speaking:

  1. Always Remember
  2. Smile
  3. Be GrateFul
  4. Prepare, Rehearse & Have a back up plan
  5. Be clear on your Intent
  6. Keep the focus on your audience
  7. Be early
  8. Visualize the outcome you want
  9. Trust
  10. Manage your Energy
